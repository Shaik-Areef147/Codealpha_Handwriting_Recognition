How it works

01
🖊️

You draw

Your digit is captured from the HTML5 canvas as a 320×320 PNG image.

02
🔬

Preprocessing

Flask resizes the image to 28×28 pixels, normalizes pixel values, and handles color inversion.

03
🧠

CNN inference

A 3-block Convolutional Neural Network trained on 60,000 MNIST samples predicts the digit.

04
📊

Confidence scores

Softmax outputs per digit are shown as a probability bar chart for full transparency.

Built with TensorFlow · Flask · Deployed on Render
